Research Interests

My coursework has focused on agroecology, sustainability, and the needs of rural agricultural regions of NM. I aspire to apply these interests to sustainable development and land use, the effects of climate change on global small agricultural communities, and conservation. My work for my internship with the city of Albuquerque focused on creating resource maps of composting facilities, and creation of a map comparing heat vulnerability and social justice in the city.
